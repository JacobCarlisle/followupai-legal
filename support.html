Support

Need help with FollowUp AI? Contact support using the details below.

Contact Support

Email: YOUR_SUPPORT_EMAIL@example.com

You can also visit the project issue page for non-private technical issues: GitHub Issues.

When contacting support, include:

  • A short description of the issue.
  • Your device model and iOS version.
  • The FollowUp AI app version.
  • Screenshots or steps that help reproduce the issue, if available.

Privacy Requests

For privacy-related requests, including access, correction, export, or deletion requests, email YOUR_SUPPORT_EMAIL@example.com with the subject line “Privacy Request.”

Account Deletion

If FollowUp AI includes account functionality, use the account deletion option in the app when available. You may also email support with the subject line “Account Deletion Request.”

Subscriptions and Billing

If you purchased FollowUp AI through the App Store, subscription management and billing are handled by Apple. You can manage subscriptions from your Apple ID account settings on your device.

AI Output

AI-generated suggestions, drafts, summaries, and reminders may be inaccurate or incomplete. Review all AI output before relying on it or sending it to another person.

Emergency Notice

FollowUp AI is not an emergency service. If you are in immediate danger or need urgent assistance, contact emergency services in your area.
