Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how FollowUp AI (“FollowUp AI,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, discloses, and protects information when you use the FollowUp AI mobile application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the app and which features are enabled, we may collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information: name, email address, sign-in identifiers, profile information, and authentication data if the app includes account creation or login.
  • User content: follow-up notes, reminders, tasks, messages, prompts, drafts, contact-related details you enter, feedback, support requests, and other content you choose to submit.
  • Integration data: information from services or device permissions you choose to connect, such as calendars, contacts, notifications, or other productivity tools. We only access these integrations when you enable the relevant feature or permission.
  • AI interaction data: prompts, instructions, context, generated responses, and related metadata needed to provide AI-powered features.
  • Usage and device information: app interactions, feature usage, device type, operating system, app version, diagnostic information, crash logs, and performance data.
  • Purchase information: subscription or purchase status as provided by Apple. We do not receive your full payment card details from Apple.

2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve FollowUp AI.
  • Create, organize, and manage follow-ups, reminders, drafts, and related app features.
  • Generate AI-powered suggestions, summaries, drafts, and other app responses.
  • Respond to support requests and communicate with users.
  • Monitor app reliability, diagnose bugs, protect security, and improve performance.
  • Prevent misuse, enforce our Terms of Use, and protect the safety and integrity of the service.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. AI Features and User Content

FollowUp AI may use third-party AI model providers or cloud service providers to process prompts, instructions, user content, and related context in order to generate app responses. Do not submit information that you do not want processed by the app or its service providers.

AI-generated content may be inaccurate, incomplete, or unsuitable for your circumstances. You are responsible for reviewing AI-generated content before relying on it or sending it to another person.

4. Sharing of Information

We may share information with:

  • Service providers: vendors that help us operate the app, such as hosting, cloud storage, authentication, analytics, crash reporting, customer support, and AI model providers.
  • Apple: for App Store purchases, subscriptions, app distribution, app analytics, and related platform services.
  • Connected services: third-party services you choose to connect or interact with through the app, subject to your permissions and those services’ terms and privacy policies.
  • Legal and safety purposes: when required by law, legal process, or to protect rights, safety, and security.
  • Business transfers: in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, reorganization, or sale of assets.

We do not sell personal information. We do not use personal information to track you across apps or websites owned by other companies unless we disclose that practice and obtain any consent required by applicable law.

5. Analytics and Diagnostics

We may use analytics and crash reporting tools to understand app usage, troubleshoot problems, and improve FollowUp AI. These tools may collect device, usage, and diagnostic information. Analytics and diagnostics are used to operate and improve the app, not to sell user data.

6. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the app, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, maintain security, and support business operations. If you request deletion, we will delete or de-identify information unless we need to retain it for legitimate legal, security, or operational reasons.

7. Your Choices and Rights

You may be able to access, update, export, or delete certain information through the app. You may also contact us to request access, correction, deletion, export, or other privacy assistance. Depending on where you live, you may have additional privacy rights under applicable law.

For privacy choices and data requests, visit Privacy Choices.

8. Device Permissions and Integrations

Some features may request access to device permissions or integrations, such as notifications, contacts, calendar data, microphone, or files. You can manage device permissions in iOS Settings. Some features may not work properly if required permissions are disabled.

9. Children’s Privacy

FollowUp AI is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can take appropriate action.

10. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. International Processing

Information may be processed and stored in the United States or other countries where we or our service providers operate. These locations may have data protection laws different from those in your jurisdiction.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the “Last updated” date and may provide additional notice as appropriate.
