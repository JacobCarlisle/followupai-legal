Privacy Choices

Last updated: May 3, 2026

This page explains how to manage privacy choices and submit data requests for FollowUp AI.

Access, Correction, Export, or Deletion Requests

To request access, correction, export, or deletion of personal information associated with your use of FollowUp AI, email jacobrcarlisle@gmail.com with the subject line “Privacy Request.”

Please include enough information for us to identify the relevant account or app data. Do not include sensitive information in a public forum or issue tracker.

Account Deletion

If FollowUp AI includes account functionality, use the account deletion option in the app when available. You may also email us with the subject line “Account Deletion Request.”

Device Permissions

You can manage iOS permissions such as notifications, contacts, calendars, microphone, and files in your device Settings. Turning off permissions may limit some app functionality.

Subscriptions

If you purchased a subscription through the App Store, subscription management, cancellation, and billing are handled by Apple through your Apple ID account settings.

Marketing and Communications

If we send optional marketing communications, you may opt out using the unsubscribe mechanism in the message or by contacting support. We may still send transactional or service-related communications.
